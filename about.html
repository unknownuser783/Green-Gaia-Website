Our Prompt

Green Gaia is a hypothetical restaurant developed for the Technology Student Association (TSA). We were tasked with creating a website for a vegetarian restaurant that showcases its approach and values. From this, we developed Green Gaia.

Real-World Application

If we were to turn our hypothetical restaurant into a real business, here's how we'd do it:

  • Partner with local farms and small businesses for produce and products
  • Host community events for awareness and funding
  • Develop a social media following
  • Donate a portion of our funds to the International Foundation for Gastrointestinal Disorders
